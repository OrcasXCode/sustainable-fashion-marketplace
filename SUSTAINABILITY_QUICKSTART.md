# Quick Start Guide - Sustainability Features

## What's New?

Your Sustainable Fashion Marketplace now has **comprehensive sustainability tracking** for every product!

## For Sellers

### How to Add Sustainability Information

1. **Login as a Seller**
   - Username: `eco_threads` or `green_wear`
   - Password: `seller123`

2. **Add New Product**
   - Click "Add Product" button
   - You'll see 4 organized sections:

#### Section 1: Basic Information
- Product name, description, price
- Material, stock quantity, image

#### Section 2: Sustainability Details
- **Raw Material Source**: Where materials come from
  - Example: "Organic cotton from Maharashtra farms"
  
- **Transportation Method**: How materials are transported
  - Example: "Local trains, 100km radius, minimal emissions"
  
- **Manufacturing Process**: How the product is made
  - Example: "Hand-picked, eco-friendly processing, GOTS certified"
  
- **Certifications**: Industry standards
  - Example: "GOTS, Fair Trade, OEKO-TEX"

#### Section 3: Environmental Impact
- **Carbon Footprint**: COâ‚‚ saved or emitted
  - Example: "2.1 kg COâ‚‚ saved per garment"
  
- **Water Usage**: Water consumption details
  - Example: "95% less water than conventional cotton"
  
- **Recyclability**: End-of-life disposal options
  - Example: "100% biodegradable, compostable"

#### Section 4: Benefits & Impact
- **Environmental Benefits**: Positive impacts
  - Example: "No toxic chemicals, improves soil health"
  
- **Usage Advantages**: Why buyers should choose this
  - Example: "Hypoallergenic, breathable, long-lasting"
  
- **Conventional Impact**: Problems with alternatives
  - Example: "Conventional cotton uses 16% of world's pesticides"

3. **Edit Existing Products**
   - Go to "My Products"
   - Look for yellow "Add Sustainability Info" badges
   - Click "Edit" to add missing information
   - Green "Sustainability Complete" badge appears when all key fields are filled
